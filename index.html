Changes in UND to select accounts balances in the last 24 hrs. Accrued rewards are UND rewards that have not been withdrawn yet.

The changes to account balances are due to UND spent, staking rewards explicitly withdrawn, deposits spent or returned on proposals.

Negative accrued rewards are rewards that have been withdrawn, and will reflect as a positive value under the balance column. There is slippage because accounts that have staked less than 100 UND are not considered.
